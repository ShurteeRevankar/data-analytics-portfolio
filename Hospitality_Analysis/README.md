## Key Insights
- Revenue dipped in June by 4.8% vs May, suggesting mid-quarter demand slack or pricing gap.
- Delhi underperforms vr its market potential - Rs 294.5M vs Mumbai's Rs 668.6M despite comparable hotel count.
- Cancellation rate of 24.83% is high - directly impacting occupancy and revenue realization.
- Weekday revenue is 67% higher than weekends, showing heavy corporate booking dependency.
- Elite rooms drive 32.79% of revenue but show 57.6% occupancy - room to upsell and improve fill rate.
- Journey & LogTrip platforms have highest realisation (70.7%) - OTAs like MakeMyTrip slightly lower.
